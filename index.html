I am a postdoc in the Internet Architecture (INET) group at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics, Saarbrücken, Germany.

My research focuses on online privacy, data protection, and network measurement — building tools and conducting empirical studies to understand how the web collects and leaks user data, from cookie consent mechanisms to advertising trackers.

I am Vietnamese and enjoy cooking and eating different kinds of food.
